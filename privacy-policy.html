When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

When you use Google Sign-In to authenticate with our service, we receive certain information from Google according to your account settings and permissions:

We use this information only to authenticate you and create your account. We do not store your Google password or full authentication credentials.

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:
